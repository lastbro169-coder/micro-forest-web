What is a Micro Forest?

A micro forest is a small, dense, fast-growing forest planted in urban areas. Even a tennis-court sized plot can become a thriving ecosystem full of native trees and plants.

Why It Matters

  • 🌬️ Cleans the air
  • 🌞 Cools the city
  • 🐝 Supports pollinators and wildlife
